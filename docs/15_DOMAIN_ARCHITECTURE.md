# DOMAIN ARCHITECTURE

This document defines the core business entities for the AI Interview Platform. We have challenged the initial proposed list to reduce unnecessary complexity and align with Domain-Driven Design (DDD) principles.

## Entity Consolidation & Refinement
*   **Renamed `Profile` to `CandidateProfile`**: Ensures clear boundaries when we introduce `RecruiterProfile` in the future.
*   **Merged `InterviewSession` into `Interview`**: A separate "Session" entity is over-engineering for V1. An `Interview` inherently has a start time, end time, and WebRTC recording URL. We don't need a separate domain concept for the network connection.
*   **Replaced `Transcript` with `TranscriptLine`**: A monolithic `Transcript` entity is hard to query. Breaking it down into `TranscriptLine` allows us to easily map specific audio timestamps to specific `Evaluations`.
*   **Renamed `CoachingPlan` to `CoachingFeedback`**: A "Plan" implies a multi-week curriculum. "Feedback" accurately reflects post-interview actionable advice.
*   **CandidateMemory expanded**: To support the Memory Confidence Model and prevent "Hallucination Poisoning", memory is no longer a generic JSON blob. It acts as an aggregate for individual `MemoryItem` entities with distinct lifecycles.

---

## 1. User
*   **Purpose**: The root identity and billing entity.
*   **Responsibilities**: Authentication, authorization, subscription management.
*   **Lifecycle**: Created on signup. Exists indefinitely unless the account is deleted.
*   **Ownership**: Independent root aggregate.

## 2. CandidateProfile
*   **Purpose**: The professional identity of the user.
*   **Responsibilities**: Storing target roles, years of experience, and general preferences.
*   **Lifecycle**: Created immediately after `User`. Updated frequently.
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `User`.

## 3. Resume
*   **Purpose**: A snapshot of the candidate's historical experience.
*   **Responsibilities**: Storing the raw file URL and the structured, parsed JSON (skills, timeline).
*   **Lifecycle**: Uploaded by user. Immutable once parsed (if they get a new job, they upload a new Resume).
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `CandidateProfile`.

## 4. JobDescription (JD)
*   **Purpose**: The target rubric for a specific interview.
*   **Responsibilities**: Storing the raw JD text and the parsed requirements (must-haves, domain skills).
*   **Lifecycle**: Created before an interview. Immutable.
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `CandidateProfile`.

## 5. CandidateMemory
*   **Purpose**: The long-term intelligence aggregate.
*   **Responsibilities**: Managing a collection of `MemoryItem` records.
*   **Lifecycle**: Created alongside `CandidateProfile`. Exists permanently.
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `CandidateProfile`.

## 6. MemoryItem
*   **Purpose**: A single tracked behavioral trait, weakness, or strength.
*   **Responsibilities**: Storing the specific trait, its state (e.g., OBSERVED, CONFIRMED, DISPUTED), and references to the interviews where it was detected.
*   **Lifecycle**: Created dynamically after an interview. Transitions states based on repeated detections or user disputes.
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `CandidateMemory`.

## 7. Interview
*   **Purpose**: The core transactional event of the platform.
*   **Responsibilities**: Tracking the state of the session (Planned -> Started -> Completed -> Evaluated), storing the recording URL, and linking the inputs (Resume, JD).
*   **Lifecycle**: Created when a user requests an interview. Mutates rapidly during the live session. Locked once completed.
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `CandidateProfile`.

## 8. InterviewPlan
*   **Purpose**: The AI's syllabus for the interview.
*   **Responsibilities**: Storing the topics to cover based on the intersection of the Resume, JD, and CONFIRMED MemoryItems.
*   **Lifecycle**: Generated asynchronously right after the `Interview` is requested. Immutable.
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `Interview`.

## 9. TranscriptLine
*   **Purpose**: Granular record of the conversation.
*   **Responsibilities**: Storing speaker (AI vs. Candidate), text, timestamps, and linking to specific generated Questions.
*   **Lifecycle**: Appended rapidly during the live `Interview`. Immutable once saved.
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `Interview`.

## 10. Evaluation
*   **Purpose**: A specific granular score against a rubric.
*   **Responsibilities**: Storing the score (e.g., 85/100), the dimension (e.g., "Clarity"), and the evidence (quote from TranscriptLine).
*   **Lifecycle**: Generated by the Evaluation Worker after the `Interview` completes. Immutable.
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `Interview`.

## 11. Report
*   **Purpose**: The aggregated scorecard presented to the user.
*   **Responsibilities**: Storing the overall score, high-level summary, and links to granular `Evaluations`.
*   **Lifecycle**: Generated after all `Evaluations` are complete. Immutable.
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `Interview`.

## 12. CoachingFeedback
*   **Purpose**: Actionable advice to improve weaknesses.
*   **Responsibilities**: Storing "What went wrong" and "How to fix it" (e.g., specific STAR method rewrites).
*   **Lifecycle**: Generated alongside the `Report`. Immutable.
*   **Ownership**: Owned by `Evaluation`.
